Our Story

τρίϕατσο is more than just a cafe - it's a celebration of coffee culture blended with contemporary flair. Our name, symbolizing three faces, represents the unity of past, present, and future in every cup we serve.

We carefully select our beans, prepare each drink with precision, and create an atmosphere where friends become family. From your morning espresso to afterwork drinks, we're here for every moment of your day. Whether you're stopping by for a quick coffee or settling in for a leisurely afternoon, τρίϕατσο is your home away from home.

Our minimalist approach to design mirrors our philosophy: quality over quantity, authenticity over trends, and warmth over formality.

Our Coffee Story

From bean to cup - a journey of quality

Carefully Sourced, Expertly Crafted

At τρίϕατσο, coffee is an art form. We partner with select roasters who share our commitment to excellence, sourcing single-origin beans from ethical farms around the world.

Our baristas are trained artisans, masters of extraction and temperature, who understand that every bean has a story to tell. From the highlands of Ethiopia to the mountains of Colombia, we bring you flavors that celebrate their origin.

We roast in small batches to ensure maximum freshness, and every espresso shot is pulled with precision timing. The result? A cup that honors the farmer, the roaster, and most importantly - you.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know

Do you have WiFi?

+

Yes! We offer free high-speed WiFi to all our guests. Perfect for remote work or catching up on emails.

Can I work from here with my laptop?

+

Absolutely! We love our laptop warriors. Comfortable seating, power outlets, and great coffee make τρίϕατσο ideal for remote work.

Do you take reservations?

+

We accept reservations for groups of 6 or more, and for all private events. Email us at trifatsoathens@gmail.com or click the "Book a Table" button.

Do you have vegan/vegetarian options?

+

Yes! We offer plant-based milk alternatives (oat, almond, soy) and have vegan snack options. Just ask our staff!

What's the Sunday DJ event?

+

Every Sunday we host live DJ sets starting at 8pm. Great music, amazing atmosphere, and special drink offers. Come experience τρίϕατσο's night vibe!

Is there parking nearby?

+

Yes, there's street parking on Ευτυχίδου and surrounding streets. The area is also well-connected by public transport.
